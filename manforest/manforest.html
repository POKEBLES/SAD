Executive Summary

Overview

Manforest Graphics needs accurate pricing, faster approvals, and real-time visibility across quotation, production, and payments. Our Job Order Management System (JOMS) centralizes operations and provides actionable analytics for better decisions.

Key Features

  • Dynamic quotation with material-based costing
  • Client portal for requests, approvals, and tracking
  • Integrated online payments (DP & balance)
  • Production status monitoring

Expected Outcomes

  • Faster quote-to-approval cycle
  • Improved cash flow via timely payments
  • Reduced miscommunication across teams
  • Data-driven pricing and demand insights

Project Details

  • Client: Manforest Graphics
  • Team: FOURMULA
  • Focus: Quotation, Tracking, Payments, Analytics
  • Tech: Web app, Chart.js analytics

Project Scope & Impact

In Scope (Features)

  • Client registration and authentication
  • Dynamic quotation engine

Out of Scope (Limitations)

  • Native iOS/Android mobile application
  • Comprehensive inventory management

  • Automated refund processing or subscriptions
  • Deep analysis limited by academic term
