Dynamic Quotation and Job Order Management System for Manforest Graphics

A Capstone Project Proposal by FOURMULA

Fiona Bordador Cailiegh Coleta Claudine Del Mundo Sophia Carmel Sevillano

The Problem

The custom signage and graphics fabrication industry is fraught with operational challenges. For Manforest Graphics, this means a daily struggle with disconnected, manual processes.

Manual Quotations

Inaccurate, time-consuming quotes due to volatile material prices and variable logistics costs (Luzon, Visayas, Mindanao) lead to lost revenue and client trust.

Disconnected Workflows

Data is siloed in spreadsheets and chat messages. This creates miscommunication between sales, design, production, and installation, leading to potential delays.

Lack of Insight

Management has difficulty tracking profitability per job, identifying production bottlenecks, or analyzing which services are most in-demand, hindering strategic planning.

The Proposed Solution

A centralized, web-based Job Order Management System (JOMS) that streamlines the entire workflow, integrates a dynamic job costing engine, and provides powerful business analytics.

Project Scope & Impact

In Scope (Features)

Out of Scope (Limitations)
